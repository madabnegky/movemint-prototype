Strategy Overview

Offers
everywhere.

Consumers are making financial decisions across more channels than ever. Movemint helps financial institutions show up in the right place, at the right time, with the right offer.

The offer exists.
The consumer has a need.
The two rarely connect.

A consumer searches for an auto loan. They have a pre-approved offer from their financial institution sitting unused. They end up financing through the dealership — often at a higher rate — and neither side knew how close they were to a better outcome.

The Vision in Practice

Every moment is a touchpoint.

Movemint connects your pre-approved offers to the channels your consumers already use — so your institution is present when a financial decision is being made.

Scenario 01 — The Search

Sarah asks an AI assistant where to get the best auto loan rate.

She's not on your website or in your app. She's using a tool she already trusts to help her make a decision — and she's going to act on what it tells her.

Movemint connects your pre-approved offer to AI retrieval layers, so when Sarah asks that question, her institution's offer is part of the response. Her rate, her limit, personalized to her.

She gets a better outcome. The institution earns the loan. That's what showing up looks like.

Scenario 02 — The Branch

Marcus comes in to make a deposit. Movemint gives the teller context they didn't have before.

Marcus has a pre-approved credit card offer — but without a prompt, that offer stays invisible. The teller wouldn't know to bring it up, and Marcus wouldn't know to ask.

When his account is pulled up, Movemint surfaces the offer directly in the CRM with the relevant details. The teller has what they need to start a natural, informed conversation.

The offer gets to the consumer. The institution captures a product relationship it might have missed.

Scenario 03 — The Dealership

Elena walks into a dealership. Her phone lets her know she's already pre-approved.

Movemint detects that Elena has entered a known auto dealership location. She has a pre-approved auto loan — but without a timely nudge, she'd likely finance through whoever the dealership suggests.

A push notification from her institution's app surfaces the offer in context. She goes into the conversation informed, with a concrete option already in hand.

This is what it means for an institution to be present at the point of decision — not just at the point of application.

Scenario 04 — The Website

David visits the website to check CD rates. The chatbot surfaces something relevant.

He came for one thing. Movemint gives the chatbot the context to offer something else — without interrupting the experience he came for.

This is how financial institutions stay engaged with consumers between major life events. Small moments, done well, build the relationship.

How It Works

One platform.
Every channel.

Movemint connects your existing offers to the channels your consumers are already using. Your team defines the offers. Movemint handles delivery and timing across every connected touchpoint.

Step 01
⚙️

Configure and Publish

Your institution defines pre-approved offers in Movemint — amount, rate, eligibility criteria. Your team stays in control of the offer. Movemint handles distribution.

Step 02
📡

Signals Come In

Location check-ins, AI queries, chatbot sessions, branch logins — every consumer touchpoint generates a signal. Movemint uses those signals to determine when and where an offer is relevant.

Step 03
⦿

Offers Reach Consumers

The right offer reaches the right consumer in the channel they're already using. Your institution shows up at the moment of decision — not after it's passed.

The Bottom Line

Consumers are ready to engage.
They just need the offer to find them.

Movemint helps financial institutions increase engagement through relevant, timely digital experiences that support measurable growth.
