Getting Started: Your First 5 Minutes

Follow these steps to get oriented with the prototype:

  1. Open the Storefront
    Click Storefront from the launchpad. This is the consumer-facing marketplace where members see their personalized offers.
  2. Look at the Preview Controls
    At the top of the page, you'll see two controls:
    • Demo/Live Toggle - Switches between demo mode (static offers) and live mode (rule-based offers)
    • Preview As Dropdown - Lets you simulate different member profiles
  3. Try Live Mode
    Click "Live" in the toggle, then select "High Credit Member (720+)" from the Preview As dropdown. Watch the offers change based on campaign rules!
  4. Switch Profiles
    Now select "Low Credit Member (<650)". Notice how fewer offers appear and some that were "Preapproved" are now "Apply Now" (or hidden entirely).
  5. Explore the Admin
    Go to Admin > Campaigns to see how campaigns and rules are configured.
💡
Pro Tip Keep two browser tabs open - one on the Storefront and one on Admin > Campaigns. Make changes in Admin, then refresh Storefront to see the results immediately.

Testing Scenarios: Step-by-Step

Use these scenarios to demonstrate how the rule engine works and how different member attributes affect offer eligibility.

🎯 Scenario 1: Credit Score Impact on Preapproval

Goal: Show how credit score affects whether a member gets preapproved vs. invited to apply.

  1. Go to Storefront and switch to Live mode
  2. Select High Credit Member (750) - look for the New Auto Loan offer
  3. Note: Should show as Preapproved with a high limit (e.g., $50,000)
  4. Now switch to Mid Credit Member (680)
  5. Note: Same product might show with a lower preapproval limit (e.g., $35,000)
  6. Finally, switch to Low Credit Member (620)
  7. Note: Product may show as Apply Now (ITA) or not appear at all
ℹ️
What's happening behind the scenes? The campaign has multiple preapproval rules with different credit score thresholds. The system evaluates each rule and uses the highest matching preapproval limit.

🚗 Scenario 2: Auto Refinance Targeting

Goal: Show how the "Has Auto Loan" attribute targets members for refinance offers.

  1. Go to Storefront in Live mode
  2. Select High Credit Member (750)
  3. Look for Auto Refinance - it should NOT appear (Has Auto Loan = No)
  4. Now switch to Auto Loan Holder (710)
  5. The Auto Refinance offer should now appear (Has Auto Loan = Yes)
  6. Also try Mid Credit Member (680) who also has an auto loan
💡
Why this matters You only want to show refinance offers to members who actually have a loan to refinance. Product rules with "Has Auto Loan is true" ensure you don't show irrelevant offers.

🏔️ Scenario 3: Credit Mountain for Low-Credit Members

Goal: Show how Credit Mountain provides a fallback experience when members don't qualify for preapprovals.

  1. First, enable the feature: Go to Admin > Feature Flags
  2. Turn ON storefront_creditMountain
  3. Go to Storefront in Live mode
  4. Select Low Credit Member (620)
  5. If no preapproved offers match, the Credit Mountain AI Coach card should appear
  6. This gives the member a path forward instead of an empty "no offers" state

⚙️ Scenario 4: Building Your Own Rule

Goal: Create a custom rule and watch it affect offer visibility in real-time.

  1. Go to Admin > Campaigns
  2. Click on "Always-On Member Offers" (perpetual campaign)
  3. Go to the Products tab
  4. Click Edit on any product (e.g., "New Auto Loan")
  5. Scroll to Product Rules - add a new rule:
    Account Balance greater_than_or_equal 10000
  6. Click Save Changes
  7. Go back to Storefront in Live mode
  8. Select High Credit Member (balance: $25,000) - offer visible
  9. Select Mid Credit Member (balance: $5,000) - offer NOT visible
⚠️
Remember to undo your changes If you're demoing for others, you may want to remove this test rule afterward so it doesn't affect future demos.

📊 Scenario 5: Demo Mode vs. Live Mode Comparison

Goal: Show the difference between static demo offers and rule-evaluated live offers.

  1. Go to Storefront
  2. Make sure you're in Demo mode (the default)
  3. Note: You see preset demo offers regardless of any profile selection
  4. Count the offers and note which ones are "Preapproved"
  5. Now switch to Live mode and select High Credit Member
  6. Notice: Offers may be different! They're now generated from campaign rules

How to Set Up a Campaign from Scratch

Follow this guide to create and configure a new campaign with products and rules.

Step 1: Create the Campaign

  1. Go to Admin > Campaigns
  2. Click + New Campaign
  3. Fill in the basics:
    • Name: e.g., "Q1 2025 Credit Card Push"
    • Type: Choose Perpetual (for always-on) or Targeted (for file-based)
    • Status: Set to "Live" when ready to test
  4. Click Create Campaign

Step 2: Add Products to the Campaign

  1. Click into your new campaign
  2. Go to the Products tab
  3. Click + Add Product in the Featured Offers section or a category section
  4. Select a product from your product catalog (e.g., "Platinum Rewards Visa")
  5. The product is now part of your campaign

Step 3: Configure Rules

  1. Click Edit on the product you just added
  2. You'll see the Product Rules Editor
  3. Product Rules (who sees the offer):
    Add a rule like: Credit Score >= 600
    Members below 600 won't see this product at all
  4. Preapproval Rules (who gets preapproved):
    Add a rule like: Credit Score >= 700 with Limit: $15,000
    Members 700+ see "Preapproved up to $15,000"
    Members 600-699 see "Apply Now" (ITA)
  5. Click Save Changes

Step 4: Test Your Campaign

  1. Go to Storefront
  2. Switch to Live mode
  3. Select different member profiles to verify your rules work correctly
  4. Check that:
    • High credit members see preapproved offers
    • Mid credit members see ITA offers
    • Low credit members don't see offers they shouldn't qualify for
💡
Quick Test Tip Keep the Storefront open in one tab and Admin in another. After saving rule changes in Admin, just refresh the Storefront to see the effects immediately.

Understanding Rule Logic

Rules are the heart of personalization. Here's how they work:

Product Rules vs. Preapproval Rules

Product Rules

Question: "Should this member see this product at all?"

If matches: Product shows as Invite to Apply (ITA)

If no match: Product is hidden

Preapproval Rules

Question: "Should this member be preapproved?"

If matches: Product shows as Preapproved (with limit if set)

If no match: Falls back to ITA (if product rules matched)

How Clauses Work Together

Within a single rule: All clauses are ANDed together
Credit Score >= 680 AND Has Auto Loan = true = Member must meet BOTH conditions
Between multiple rules: Rules are ORed together
Rule 1: Credit Score >= 750
Rule 2: Member Since >= 5 years
= Member needs to meet EITHER Rule 1 OR Rule 2

Example: Complex Rule Setup

Product: Premium Auto Loan

Product Rules (who sees it):
  Rule 1: Credit Score >= 650
  Rule 2: Member Since >= 3 AND Account Balance >= 5000

  → Member sees product if they have 650+ credit
    OR if they've been a member 3+ years with $5k+ balance

Preapproval Rules (who gets preapproved):
  Rule 1: Credit Score >= 720  →  Limit: $50,000
  Rule 2: Credit Score >= 680  →  Limit: $35,000

  → 720+ gets preapproved at $50k
  → 680-719 gets preapproved at $35k
  → Below 680 but meets product rules = ITA

Common Questions

Q: Why don't I see any offers in Live mode?

A: You need to select a member profile. In Live mode, the system evaluates rules against a specific member's attributes. Use the "Preview As" dropdown to select a profile.

Q: I changed a rule but the Storefront didn't update?

A: Make sure you:

  1. Clicked "Save Changes" in the rule editor
  2. The campaign status is "Live" (not Draft or Pending)
  3. Refreshed the Storefront page
  4. Are in "Live" mode (not Demo mode)

Q: What's the difference between a Product and a Campaign Product?

A: Products (in Admin > Products) are your catalog - the master list of all loan/deposit products. Campaign Products are instances of those products added to a specific campaign, where you configure rules for that campaign.

Q: Can a product be in multiple campaigns?

A: Yes! The same product can be in multiple campaigns with different rules. In Live mode, the system aggregates offers from ALL live campaigns and de-duplicates, keeping the best offer for each product.

Q: How do I reset everything to start fresh?

A: Open your browser's developer tools (F12), go to Application > Local Storage, and delete all keys starting with movemint_. Then refresh the page.
